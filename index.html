About Us

The Multimodal Cognition research group (MultiCog) at BITS Pilani is a group of researchers led by Dr. Pratik Narang. MultiCog focuses on leveraging multimodal information and Artificial Intelligence to analyse and build real-world systems for Computer Vision, Cyber Security, IoT and Healthcare Applications.

Our research aims to address key problems in Artificial Intelligence by closely studying its underlying fundamental aspects, and proposing novel solutions for enhanced performance. As the research in AI becomes more unprecedented than ever before, we strive to augment this growth by building algorithms that are highly perceptive, efficient and robust. Our group explores frontiers in various fields such as complete image perception, vision in adverse conditions, remote sensing and aerial image analysis, adversarial machine learning, privacy preserving machine learning, fake news detection, etc. We seek to address these challenges by exploiting the efficacy of neural networks and machine optimization to build intelligent and adept systems.
